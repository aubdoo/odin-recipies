Description

This is Odin's Own Lasanga. The recipie was taguht to him by his mother atop the slopes of the tallest scandinavian mountain. After his mother's passing, he worked for years to perfect it, experimenting with new ingredients and testing it in well-known viking halls. Now, you can make it too! This cheezy, meat-filled delicacy will feed the whole family! Including some leftovers too!

Are you worried making a lasagna will be too difficult? Don't be! I'll be there to walk you all the way through it. In just a few east steps, you'll have an amazing lasagna cooking in your oven. Just gtab a 9"x13" pan and let's get started!

Ingredients

  • Meat: You will need 3/4 cup of both mild italian sausage, and ground beef.
  • Onion and Garlic: One onion and two cloves of garlic to be cooked into the meat.
  • Tomato Products: One can of crushed tomatoes and ~40 oz of tomato sauce. (Lasagna is a labor of love, so consider high quality sauce like Rao's!)
  • (Optional) Sugar: Two tablespoons of sugar for a subtle sweet flavor for the sauce.
  • Spices and seasoning: 4 tbsp of parsley, basil leaves, 1 1/2 tsp salt, 1 tsp italian seasoning, 1/4 tsp fennel seeds, and 1/4 tsp black pepper.
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses: 3/4 cup Fresh grated parmasean, 3/4 lb sliced mozzarella, and 2 cups of ricotta.
  • Egg: Binds the ricotta cheese mixture.

Steps

1. Cheese mixture

  • Combine cheeses
  • Add egg
  • Add pepper, italian seasoning,1/4 tsp salt
  • Stir

2. Meat sauce

  1. Heat olive oilve oil over medium heat.
  2. Add diced onions. Let them soften for 10 minutes.
  3. Add meats. cook medium-high for 8-10 minutes. add garlic during the last minute

3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees

4. Cook Noodles

  1. Follow cooking instructions on desired noodle box
  2. Drain and rinse noodles with cold water
  3. Lay on parchment paper to cool off

5. Assembly

  1. Spread a layer of meat sauce. Add 4 noodles
  2. Spread 1/3 of the cheese mixture, another layer of meat sauce,
  3. Add 4 noodles, 1/3 of the cheese mixture, and another layer of meat sauce.
  4. Finish with 4 more noodles, the rest of the cheese mix and the rest of the meat sauce.
  5. Top with a layer of Mozzarella cheese

Bake

  • Bake for 35-45 min
  • Remove from oven, and let it sit for 15 min

Enjoy!
