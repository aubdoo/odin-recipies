Odin's Own Pancakes

Description

Love pancakes? Well in today's new recipie, Odin's got you covered. We are going to be making Odin's Own Pancakes!

A traditional pancake recipie that Odin learned and perfected during his time serving the Jarl and his housecarls as a breakfast chef! The Jarl loved it, so I think your kids will too!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 3 tbsp melted butter
  • 1 large egg

Steps

1. Mix

  1. Combine flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in a large bowl
  2. Make a well in the center. Add milk, melted butter, and egg. Mix until smooth.

2. Cook

  1. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or pan over medium-high heat
  2. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake; cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry, about 2 to 3 minutes.
  3. Flip and cook until browned on the other side. Repeat with remaining batter.

Enjoy!
